Welcome to Computer Science!

The goal of this website is to help you decide if Computer Science is right for you, and shed some light about some of the things Kent State offers for people who choose this degree path.

Languages - Popular coding languages used in the industry of Computer Science today.

Community - Clubs and activities for Kent State Computer Science students, or other students interested in doing fun projects in their free time.

Concentrations - Links and descriptions of the concentrations offered for the Bachelors of Computer Science at Kent State.
