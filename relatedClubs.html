KSU Math

A club entirely about math! They promote and enjoy math-themed activities and host a number of similarly math-themed events on campus, such as a math-themed footrace and the famous "mathemagician" Arthur Benjamin. A link to their Discord: https://discord.gg/9QytbJs

The ATR Lab

ATR stands for Advanced Telerobotics Research. A club for people interested in robotics, the blockchain, Extended Reality and 3D printing! Welcome to people of all levels of skill and knowledge. A link to their Discord: https://discord.gg/cNqUXPPQ5v

HacKSU

HacKSU is a club that meets weekly, teaching interesting coding concepts and projects. They also host a large hackathon event, Kent Hack Enough. They have a website: https://hacksu.com/ and a Discord: https://discord.gg/rJDdvnt

KSU VGC

A club all about video games! A safe space for gamers of all sorts, from console to mobile to PC, where they can meet, talk about and play some of their favorite games. A link to their Discord: https://discord.gg/dHxrk6G
