Here are a few activities hosted by KSU Students:

Related Clubs Activities Discord
Saturday Hangout

Most every Saturday during the school semester, on the fourth floor of the Kent State Library (or virtually if the weather isn't nice), KSU CS students host a hangout! From about 10am - 2pm, students can get together and work on homework and ask for help, play games, or just chat!

Kent Hack Enough

Hosted by the fine folk at HacKSU, typically in the fall semester, Kent Hack Enough is our very own hackathon! A hackathon is a weekend-long coding event where people get together and have 24 hours to code a tech-related project. There are also talks, recruiters, and free goodies!

Tutoring

Tutoring by the students, for the students! Both virtually via Blackboard (though this may be subject to change if KSU is completely switching to Canvas) and in-person in MSB (the Math and Sciences Building) 243. Tutoring is typically offered for most core classes but tutors will help with all they can!
